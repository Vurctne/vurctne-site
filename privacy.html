2. Information we collect

We collect only what we need to run the game and improve it. Categories:

Information you provide

  • Display name, avatar, and region shared by the host platform (WeChat, Douyin, TikTok) when you open our game and grant permission.
  • In-game actions you choose to perform (e.g. submitting a score to the leaderboard, sharing a replay).
  • Messages you send us by email.

Information collected automatically

  • Platform-assigned identifiers (e.g. openid on WeChat / Douyin, anonymous user id on TikTok) used to save your progress.
  • Device and environment information provided by the host platform: device model, operating system, system language, screen size, network type, and platform app version. We do not collect your IMEI, MAC address, precise GPS location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.
  • Gameplay telemetry: game events (start, death, revive, level reached), session length, crash reports, and performance metrics.
  • Technical logs generated by our backend when your device contacts it (IP address, request timestamp, HTTP status). IP addresses are retained only for security and abuse prevention and are truncated after 30 days.

Information we do not collect

We do not ask for or collect your real name, phone number, postal address, payment card details, government ID, biometric data, or any "special category" / sensitive personal data as defined under GDPR Article 9.

3. How we use your information

  • To run and maintain the game — save your progress, rank your score, sync cloud data across devices.
  • To operate social features you choose to use — leaderboards, friend rankings, shared replays.
  • To improve gameplay and balance — aggregated analytics on what levels are too hard, where players drop off, which patterns cause crashes.
  • To prevent cheating, abuse, and fraud — detect impossibly high scores, client tampering, and automated play.
  • To respond to your support enquiries.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. Legal bases for processing (EEA / UK)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we rely on the following bases under the GDPR / UK GDPR:

  • Contract — to deliver the game you are playing (Art. 6(1)(b)).
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the game secure, measure performance, and prevent fraud, balanced against your rights (Art. 6(1)(f)).
  • Consent — where the host platform requires us to request explicit authorisation before accessing certain data (Art. 6(1)(a)). You can withdraw consent at any time in platform settings.
  • Legal obligation — where we must retain or disclose data to comply with law (Art. 6(1)(c)).

5. Sharing with third parties

We do not sell personal information. We share it only with:

  • Host platforms — WeChat (Tencent), Douyin (ByteDance), TikTok (TikTok Ltd.) — as required to run a mini-game on their platform. Their privacy policies apply to data they collect directly.
  • Cloud infrastructure — Tencent Cloud (for WeChat / Douyin backend) and Cloudflare (for this website). These providers process data on our behalf under written agreements.
  • Ad networks — the host platform's own ad SDK (e.g. Douyin rewarded video) serves ads inside the game. We do not share your identifiers with third-party ad networks beyond the host platform.
  • Legal and safety — law enforcement, regulators, or other parties where required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.
  • Business transfers — if our business is sold or merged, data may transfer to the acquirer under equivalent protections.

6. Advertising

Our games show rewarded video and interstitial ads supplied by the host platform's ad SDK. Watching an ad is always optional and used to earn in-game rewards (extra lives, energy). We do not use third-party behavioural advertising cookies or cross-app tracking.

7. International transfers

We are based in Australia. Our cloud infrastructure is operated in Australia and, for WeChat / Douyin distribution, in mainland China. If you play from the EEA, UK, or US, your data will be transferred outside your country. For EEA / UK users, we rely on the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or the UK IDTA, as applicable, for such transfers.

8. Data retention

  • Game progress and leaderboard entries — for as long as your account on the host platform remains active, plus 12 months.
  • Technical logs — 30 days.
  • Support emails — 24 months after the case is closed.
  • Records we must keep for tax or legal reasons — as required by Australian law (typically 7 years).

When the retention period ends, data is deleted or irreversibly anonymised.

9. Security

We protect your information with TLS encryption in transit, access controls on our backend, least-privilege service accounts, server-side score validation and anti-cheat checks, and regular review of dependencies for known vulnerabilities. No system is perfectly secure; we will notify affected users and regulators of a data breach as required by law.

10. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your information ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Data portability — receive your information in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g. the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner, or an EU supervisory authority).

To exercise any of these rights, email vurctne@gmail.com with enough detail for us to locate your record (typically the host platform you use and the approximate time you started playing). We will respond within 30 days and will not charge a fee unless the request is manifestly unfounded or excessive.

11. Children's privacy

Our games are not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in jurisdictions where that is the applicable age of digital consent). We do not knowingly collect personal information from such children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

12. California residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, the purpose of collection, and the categories of third parties it is shared with; to request deletion; to correct inaccurate information; and to opt out of any "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information. We do not sell personal information as the term is defined under the CCPA, and we do not share personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising. To exercise your rights, email vurctne@gmail.com. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the new version on this page and update the "Last updated" date. If changes are material, we will give prominent notice in the game or by email.
